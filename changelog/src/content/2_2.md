This version was tested on:

+ Navidrome version: [0.60.3](https://github.com/navidrome/navidrome/releases/tag/v0.60.3)
+ iOS version: 18.6 & 26.3

## 🍿 New Features & Enhancements

+ **CarPlay Support**: flo is now on your car thanks to [@docmeth02](https://github.com/docmeth02)
+ **Identity-Aware Proxy support**: Previously flo couldn't connect when Navidrome was behind an Identity-Aware Proxy, mow it can thanks to [@Piekay](https://github.com/Piekay)

## 🐞 Bug Fixes

+ "Unable to Purchase flo+" popup every time you go into Preferences. Next time you'll actually be able to purchase flo+ (still learning the integration)

---

Regarding flo+, I have mentioned this from the first day flo was released on the website: it's opt-in, nice-to-have, unnecessary features that are not related to your listening experience but may make it more fun. flo is free and open source under the MIT license, and always will be.

In the future, flo will have 2 in-app purchases:

+ "tipjar": just like the name says. You can only purchase this once
+ "flo+": you can purchase this as many times as you want

If flo has made your drives, commutes, or late-night listening sessions a little better, consider to test the purchase feature (lol)

Either way, it means more than you'd think!
